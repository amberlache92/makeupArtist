How far In advance should I book for my wedding
Typically people book as far as a year in advance for their wedding. The sooner you schedule the better it is for you. Bridal sappointments should be booked no late than 6 months before your big day.
Is there a travel fee?
I travel up to 25 miles within the 45202 zipcode with no charge. Additional charge varies depending on the distance.I do all appointments on location.
Can You also do hair for ym wedding?
Unfortunately that is not in my list of strong skills. However I am able to direct you to hairstylists that you can enlist for your special day. Hair should done before the makeup application process because you do not want to mess up your makeup when you change.
How do I prepare for my makeup application?
Make sure that you exfoilate and wash your face up 2 hours before and make sure you have a good skin care routine the weeks prior to the appointment date. No products should be added prior to the makep application for the best possible results. Wearing clothes that either button or zip up would be most apporptiate to not mess up the makeup.
How do I book an appointment?
To book an appointment you can call or email me.I typically respond within 48 hours to discuss what your looking for. All appointments require a deposit.
