Bio

Im a Cincinnati native who has a passion for makeup that started when I was a toddler. After graudating from Brown Aveda Institute I began my free lance makeup career .

I spent a year living in Morocco, there I pick up a love for henna.The beauty and intricies of the designs mesmerized me so I have spent 3 years also crafting my henna skills. Henna can add an amazing flair that can be paired with gorgeous makeup regardless of the occaison.

I do professional makeup for all ocassions including: bridal, prom, photo shoots. I keep current with trends abling me to give my clients the day they have always dreamed. I love creating flawless looks to keep my clients looking their very best for all their events.

I love Abby Girl cupcakes, Game of Thrones, coming up with new makeup looks, spending hours inside of my favorite store in the world, Ulta. I also love testing out henna designs on myself and my roommate.
